MEJO 187 Grade Estimator

Use the fields below to enter grades. If the grade has been graded and returned, check "Graded". You will see live updates for your Calculated, Projected, Worst, and Best grades. Read each respective card to learn more.

Calculated Grade

The grades entered above graded status are multiplied by respective weight and summed.

Projected Grade

An estimated final grade based on your average performance on graded items.

Worst Grade

An estimated grade if all ungraded items received a 0.

Best Grade

An estimated grade if all ungraded items received a 100.
